- Completed step: accent blue-600 fill + white `CheckCircle` lucide icon (14px)
- Active step: white fill + 2px accent blue-600 border + step number (12px/600 accent text)
- Upcoming step: gray-100 fill + gray-300 border + step number (12px/400 muted text)

- Step label: below node; 12px/600 accent (active), 12px/400 muted (others)

}

- under_review → step 2 (index 1)
- ready_for_ref_check → step 3 (index 2)
- ready_for_final_review → step 4 (index 3)
